The Signature Course (UGS 302 and 303) introduces first-year students to the university’s academic community through the exploration of new interests. The Signature Course is your opportunity to engage in college-level thinking and learning.
UT designs a variety of courses by matching student interests with faculty expertise from every college and school at the university. The result is a common intellectual experience for all first-year students.
Signature Course 是 core curriculum 的一部分，顾名思义就是介绍教授各种各样的研究，一般 不接受抵换外来学分。此课选项非常多，课程内容和难度都差别很大。“一念天堂，一念地狱” 大概说的就是如此。 UGS由302和303分类，302一般是18个学生以下的小课堂，303是由UT比较有经验的教授来讲课，学生人数大概是50到200个。
*评分：1分为不满意，5分为满意
